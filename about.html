Our Story

Born from a love of Japanese tradition and the art of perfect caramelization, KREMI brings authentic brûlée crêpes to the heart of Singapore.

Where it all began

KREMI was founded on a simple idea: to bring the authentic taste of Japanese brûlée crêpes to Singapore, crafted with the same care and precision found in the streets of Tokyo.

Crafted with care

Every crêpe we make is a celebration of craftsmanship. From our delicate, thin crêpe shells to the perfectly torched caramelized sugar crust, we believe in doing things the right way.

Hand-torched perfection

Each crêpe is finished with a caramelized brûlée crust, torched right in front of you for that signature crack and warmth. No shortcuts, just pure quality.

Our commitment to quality

We source the finest ingredients, train our team to perfection, and create an atmosphere where every visit feels special. Our recipes are inspired by traditional Japanese crêpe shops, using thin, delicate shells and premium fillings that honor the craft.

This isn't just dessert — it's an experience. A moment to slow down, savor something special, and share it with the people who matter most.

Ready to try?

Visit us at Orchard Central and experience the difference of freshly torched Japanese brûlée crêpes. Whether you're stopping by for a quick treat or sharing with friends, we can't wait to serve you.
